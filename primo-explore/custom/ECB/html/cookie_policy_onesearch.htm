Onesearch cookie POLICY

OneSearch uses cookies that facilitate the use of the search engine.

WHAT IS A COOKIE?

Cookies are small text files that record your preferences during your browser activities and are saved on your own computer or device.

USE OF COOKIES in onesearch

Cookies can on the one hand be functional. These cookies record certain of the choices and settings made on your first visit to allow you to navigate more efficiently, quickly and simply on subsequent visits. These cookies record for example preference settings and login names.

On the other hand, cookies can also be used to record visit and click activity. This can be used to make anonymized reports of the use of OneSearch.

CHANGING BROWSER SETTINGS

You can enable or delete cookies on your computer through your browser settings. The way in which this works varies from browser to browser. Consult your browsers help function if necessary. You must always make this adjustment per browser and per computer!

Not accepting, disabling or deleting cookies may result in certain functionalities of the OneSearch website being no longer accessible or operating normally. This may make your website experience unsatisfactory.
