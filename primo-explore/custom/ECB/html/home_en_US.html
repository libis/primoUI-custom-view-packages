What does OneSearch cover?

OneSearch contains print and electronic information resources which can be searched in one click.

Signing-in with your Library username and password will allow you to:

  • Request items directly via the system
  • Check your loans and requests, and
  • Save your results and queries

Search tips
AND Find results with both words
Example: ECB AND banknotes
OR Find results with one of several words.
Example: state OR sovereign
NOT Find results that exclude a word
Example: celtic NOT Irish
? Add a question mark as a placeholder for one character.
Example: wom?n will retrieve woman and women
* For truncated searches, use an asterisk
Example: mone* will retrieve money, monetary, monetaire
" " Find results with the exact phrase
Example: "Sovereign debt"
