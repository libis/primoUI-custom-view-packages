The Jesuit Armarium of the European Low Countries

A virtual portal or discovery platform to the documentary heritage of the Flemish and Dutch Jesuit provinces. Trace all the institutions precious books, manuscripts, journals, etc. to their current place of safekeeping.

Institutions which are already partaking in this platform and which hold a significant number of volumes of the Jesuit’s library, or armarium in old Latin - hence the platform’s name -, are:

  • KU Leuven Libraries – Maurits Sabbe Library
  • KADOC – KU Leuven
  • Bibliotheek Ruusbroecgenootschap

For more information, please contact jesuitarmarium@kuleuven.be
