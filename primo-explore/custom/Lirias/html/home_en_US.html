Welcome to the digital repository for KU Leuven Association research!

Lirias is a document repository to archive the KU Leuven Association research output in a digital way. Lirias captures, stores, indexes, preserves, and distributes digital research material of the KU Leuven Association.

The repository is structured into communities and collections, representing groups, departments and divisions. You can browse through the archive, search in the full text, etc.

My Lirias KU Leuven Association users can log in with their regular user-id and password on the "My Lirias" page.
